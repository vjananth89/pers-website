About Me

While I was a grad student at the State University of New York - Albany, I worked on several projects Dr. Feng Chen and his “Twitter based PSL Search Engine” research group. I also worked on several of my own projects where I tried to process and analyze large scale datasets using distributed open source technologies such as Cassandra, Elasticsearch, and TitanDB  to better understand complex data problems that exist today and how they can be solved in the future. I am extremely passionate about Big Data and the way it would change the way we approach problems in Computer Science.

I worked in an agile team that developed applications using Java/J2EE, Oracle, WebFOCUS, Cognos BI, and ServiceNow Change Management Systems.

Web Redesign Intern

I worked as a Web Redesign Intern for the Research Foundation for the State University of New York from June 2014 to May 2015. During my internship, I designed their new responsive interface, information architecture and the migration of their back end servers.
