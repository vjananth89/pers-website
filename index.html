About Me

I’m currently a Graduate student at the State University of New York - Albany, working on projects under Dr. Feng Chen and his “Twitter based PSL Search Engine” research group. I am also working on several of my own projects where I am trying to process and analyze large scale data using distributed open source technologies such as Cassandra, Elasticsearch, and Titan  to better understand how complex data problems that exist today can be solved in the future. I am extremely passionate about Big Data and the radical changes that it promises to bring to the way we approach problems in Computer Science.
